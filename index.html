Driving on Praslin Island

Exploring Praslin by car is one of the easiest and most enjoyable ways to get around. The island drives on the left-hand side (the UK system),
and traffic is typically calm and manageable. Roads are generally in good condition, though some can be narrow and winding,
especially in elevated areas—so a steady, careful approach is best. Be mindful of pedestrians, cyclists, and wildlife,
particularly near beaches and quieter areas.
Speed limits are clearly posted and should always be followed. With a little awareness,
driving here is straightforward and gives you access to places you might otherwise miss.


The best of Praslin

Praslin offers a perfect mix of relaxation, adventure, and natural beauty. Visit the famous Vallée de Mai, a UNESCO-listed nature reserve home to the iconic coco de mer palms.
Spend your days unwinding on stunning beaches like Anse Lazio and Anse Georgette, known for their crystal-clear waters and soft sand.
With your own car, you can explore charming local spots, scenic coastal roads, and hidden viewpoints at your own pace. Head to Côte d'Or for great food,
water activities, and beautiful sunset views. Wherever your interests lie, Praslin has something to offer—and DC Car Hire helps you experience it all freely.
